## 2025-05-27 - [Scoped HTML Cleaning]
**Learning:** Cleaning the entire HTML DOM (using find_all + regex) before content extraction is wasteful. Scoping the cleaning process to the identified content subtree reduced processing time by ~36% in benchmarks.
**Action:** Always prioritize content selection before applying expensive tree-traversal cleaning operations.
